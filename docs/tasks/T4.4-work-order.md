# Work Order — T4.4: Coverage, abstention, tier faithfulness, calibration

**Owner:** Track C · **Type:** [code] · **Effort:** M · **Status:** ratified
**Precedence:** this work order > `CLAUDE.md`; CONTRACT Part 3 abstention and
tier rules are normative.

## Objective

Report the cost of fail-closed behavior instead of hiding abstentions behind
answered-subset accuracy.

## Deliverables

- `src/cobol_archaeologist/eval/calibration.py`
- `tests/test_calibration.py`

## Reports

- Full-coverage T1–T3 values.
- Answer rate and answered-subset accuracy/F1.
- Abstention reason and budget-exhaustion distributions.
- T4 faithfulness separately for executed, static, and entailment tiers.
- Confidence calibration by fixed bins, Brier score, and expected calibration
  error. Abstentions have no confidence and are reported as coverage, not
  silently assigned zero.
- Attempted-but-unavailable tier counts from the complete tier ladder.

## Gates

Hand-computed fixtures cover all-abstain, no-abstain, empty tier, unavailable
Tier 1, and confidence-bin boundaries. No aggregate faithfulness may be emitted
without the per-tier table alongside it.
