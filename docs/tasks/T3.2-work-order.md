## Refresh phase — rerun on the T2.5-expanded corpus (2026-07-21)

**Why:** T2.5 grew the frozen retrieval fixture from 1,824 chunks / 7 documents
to **2,361 chunks / 8 documents** (old-side KYC 2016 + amendment docs, added for
the T6 pairs). The committed Gate B table was computed on the 7-doc corpus and
was stale. This was a rerun + refresh + re-promote — no logic changed in
`rag/{index,embed}.py`, and `tests/fixtures/retrieval/queries.jsonl` was not
touched (all 24 gold `record_id`s still resolve against the 21-record
`clauses.jsonl`).

Evidence below was regenerated by `build_relevance_report()` against the current
fixture; `pytest tests/test_retrieval.py -m network` is 4/4 green and the
offline set 9/9. **The bar held: `hybrid_rerank` mrr@5 0.744 > `dense` 0.588,
and ≥ on hit@1/hit@3.**

### The three findings called out at the 7-doc review

| # | Question | 7-doc | 8-doc | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| a | Does dense still trail BM25? | 0.592 vs 0.635 | 0.588 vs 0.612 | **Yes** — gap narrows (0.043 → 0.024) but direction holds. |
| b | Is the reranker still a near-wash over hybrid? | +0.007 mrr@5, hit@1 tie | +0.056 mrr@5, +0.083 hit@1 | **No — this reversed.** See below. |
| c | Is q23 still a universal miss? | miss in all 4 modes | miss in all 4 modes | **Yes**, unchanged. |

Finding (b) is the headline change and the reason the refresh was worth running.
Tripling the distractors degraded `hybrid` (mrr@5 0.737 → 0.689, hit@1 0.625 →
0.542) while `hybrid_rerank` held at **exactly** 0.744 / 0.625 / 0.875 across
both corpora. The reranker was not adding much when the fusion candidates were
already clean; it is what absorbs the added distractors now. The near-wash
reading from the 7-doc run was an artifact of an easy corpus, not a property of
the reranker — worth remembering before anyone proposes dropping it for latency.

Per-query movement is consistent with that story: the losses are concentrated in
the lexical modes against the new KYC-2016 text (q24 bm25 5 → miss and hybrid
1 → 2; q20 bm25 2 → 3; q15 bm25 2 → 3; q17 dense 3 → 4).

q23 (KYC-ckycr-update) missing in every mode across both corpora is now a
two-run standing result rather than a one-off — carry it into T3.3 as the
motivating case for HyDE.
